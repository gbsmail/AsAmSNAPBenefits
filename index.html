The Number of Asian American Households Using SNAP Benefits in NYC in 2021, By Borough

This number is actually lower than it was in 2020, due to a variety of pandemic-era federal aid programs, said Sophie Collyer, a doctoral student from the Center on Poverty and Social Policy at Columbia University.
