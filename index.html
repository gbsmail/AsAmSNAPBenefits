Asian American Households On Food Stamps in NYC in 2021

According to the American Community Survey, over 70,000 Asian American households in New York City relied on food stamps in 2021. This number is actually lower than it was in 2020, thanks to a variety of pandemic-era federal aid programs, said Sophie Collyer, a doctoral student from the Center on Poverty and Social Policy at Columbia University.
